PLATFORM DESCRIPTION
What is CRU?
CRU (Civic Reputation & Understanding Network) is a structured civic discourse platform that enables users to engage in informed discussions across a broad spectrum of civic topics, including:
	•	Civic issues and local governance
	•	Public policy analysis and debates
	•	Academic and educational discussions
	•	Social topics and community issues
	•	Regional and hyperlocal matters
	•	Political discussions (as one category among many)
	•	Knowledge sharing and evidence-based dialogue
Platform Neutrality Statement
CRU operates as a neutral technology intermediary platform. CRU does not endorse, promote, or oppose any political parties, candidates, public policies, or ideological positions. The platform facilitates user expression in accordance with applicable Indian law and platform policies.
CRU is designed to support structured, credibility-weighted civic communication—not to serve as a political mobilization platform, election influence infrastructure, or political campaigning tool.
